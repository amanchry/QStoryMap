 No server required: exports layers as local XYZ tiles.
 Story sections: optional narrative sections that guide readers through the map.
 One‑click publishing: publish your story map directly to GitHub Pages from QGIS.

 Example story map — a live example of what you can publish with QStoryMap: https://amanchry.github.io/kenya-health-access/
